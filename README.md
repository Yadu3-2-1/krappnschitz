### Project Description  
A fun, simple web application that generates a humorous and random past and future life prediction based on user input. The predictions are written in Malayalam, adding a local and comical touch. Just enter your details, click the button, and get ready for a laugh!        

  ### The Problem (that doesn't exist) 
To know the Past and Future 
    
### The Solution (that nobody asked for)  
Our platform is the groundbreaking solution to this utterly fabricated problem. By using a highly advanced randomization algorithm to cross-reference user input with a database of whimsical scenarios, we provide the definitive, non-factual answers to life's least important questions, finally bringing clarity to those haunted by their past
